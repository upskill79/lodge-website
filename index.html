Our Story

A Tranquil Retreat in the Heart of Tuljapur

Nestled in the sacred town of Tuljapur, Sudarshan Lodge offers a warm and comfortable stay for pilgrims and travellers visiting the renowned Tulja Bhavani Temple and surrounding areas of Dharashiv (Osmanabad) district.

With clean, well-appointed rooms, genuine hospitality, and a peaceful atmosphere, we ensure every guest feels at home. Whether you're here for darshan, business, or simply exploring the beauty of Maharashtra, Sudarshan Lodge is your ideal base.
